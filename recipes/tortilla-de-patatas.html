Tortilla de Patatas

Description

Aka 'Spanish Omelette', this popular dish from Spain is easy to make using just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 3 medium Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • 1 yellow or white onion
  • 6 Eggs
  • 2 teaspoons sea salt

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Peel and thinly slice potatoes; roughly chop onion; add to skillet ensuring they are mostly covered with olive oil. Season with 1/2 tsp sea salt. Cook on medium-high for 8-12 minutes, turning occasionaly until potatoes are tender.
  3. Drain poatoes, reserving oil. Season with more salt as needed. Let cool for a few minutes.
  4. Beat eggs in a bowl with about 1/2 tsp sea salt. Add potato and onion, tossing to ensure coated with eggs.
  5. Add a daash of oil to the bottom of a large skillet over high heat. Pour egg and potato mixture, cooking for 1 minute. Reduce heat to medium-ow and cook for approximately 5-minutes.
  6. Use a rubber spatula along the edges to ensure it's not sticking and maintain its form.
  7. Once edges of omelette are firm (center will still be a little runny), place a large plate over the skillet and to flip onto it. Gently slide back onto the pan to cook the topside of the omelette.
  8. After flipping, cook on high heat for 1 minute, then low heat for 2-3 more minutes. Use a knife or fork to check the center - it will be done when this comes out clean.
  9. Flip the tortilla de patata back onto a plate and serve warm.
